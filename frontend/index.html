Welcome to Mini DeFi

Getting Started

Mini DeFi is a multi-asset lending pool where you can deposit crypto assets to earn interest, or borrow against your collateral.

1

Connect Your Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button and approve the connection in MetaMask. Make sure you're on the correct network.

2

Deposit Assets

Select an asset from the dropdown, enter the amount you want to deposit, and confirm the transaction. Your deposit earns interest over time.

3

Borrow Against Collateral

Once you've deposited collateral, you can borrow other assets up to your collateral limit. Monitor your health factor to avoid liquidation.

4

Repay & Withdraw

Repay your borrowed amount plus interest anytime. Withdraw your deposits when they're not being used as collateral.

Key Concepts

  • Health Factor: Ratio of collateral to debt. Below 1.0 means you can be liquidated.
  • Collateral Factor: Percentage of deposit value you can borrow against.
  • Utilization: Percentage of pool deposits currently borrowed.
  • Liquidation: If health factor drops below 1.0, others can repay your debt and claim collateral with a bonus.
